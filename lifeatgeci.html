ADVAYA

Advaya is the National level Multi-fest of Government Engineering college Idukki.It's a platform to explore our technical skills.In association with advaya we were able to give colour to the dreams of students and people in lower section of our society by organising various social events such as oruvettam,kudukka etc.
Advaya on Instagram

KAVETTAM

Kavettam is the Arts day celebration of Government engineering college idukki.Its an opportunity for every GECIans to highlight their creative ambition and drive and to give them a chance to showcase their hard work and inspiration.
Kavettam on Instagram

UNION DAY

Its a massive program in which our union takes its position officially and declares to do their duty effectively.A number of programs are organised by the union which makes the day a remembering one. It's also a day for us students to show our talents onstage with tons of happiness. Non stop laughs screaming applausing during programs makes the day wonderful. Special programs are organised in the night too.

FRESHERS DAY / VARAVELPPU

Freshers day, as the name suggests is the day for the newcomers. First years are given the oppurtunity to show their talents with their classmates and made to compete for the freshers day trophy. Seniors will be there to 'encourage' you.

COLLEGE DAY / BON VOYAGE

Usually the last program of the academic year, Bon Voyage is a tribute to the final years of the college, where they are gifted the best day of their college days. With no difference in departments, the final years come together to enjoy their last days in the college.

SPORTS DAY

Usually conducted in the even semesters, sports day is held at Kulamavu Navodhaya Vidhyalaya Ground, where all 5 departments compete for the trophy. It usually spans 2 days, where first day is for athletics and second is for games, which includes football, cricket, badminton, volleyball, table tennis and much more.

above are some of the programs conducted by the college union every year.
besides that, SFI GECI also conducts dozens of other programs every year, from special programs for first years to valentines day programs, you will always have something to enjoy in the college.
